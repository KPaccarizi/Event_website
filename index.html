About The Devfest

DevFest is a global developer conference organized by Google Developer Groups (GDG) around the world. It is a celebration of the developer community and a chance to learn about the latest Google technologies. DevFests feature talks, workshops, and hands-on activities for developers of all levels.

  • Technical Talks and Workshops
  • Networking Opportunities
  • Codelabs and Hands-on Activities
  • Product Demonstrations
  • Community Building

Why You Should Join?

DevFest is an amazing way to learn about the best developer tools that Google has to offer.
It is also a great way to connect and network with fellow professionals.

Great Speakers

Attend technical sessions and presentations led by Google engineers and industry experts. These sessions cover a wide range of topics, from new product announcements to deep dives into specific Google tools and other technologies.

New technologies

New technologies is what fascinates every developer. Check out the latest Google products and services in the showcase area. Developers can meet with speakers, learn about their topics and products, and give feedback. DevFest will show you the newest and coolest tech stuff.
